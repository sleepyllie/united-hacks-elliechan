Herbal Remedies

Teas

Peppermint: Antiviral properties, helps soothe pains, throat, inflammation, and reduce congestion.

Chamomile: Reduces cold symptoms, reduces inflammation, and boosts the immune system.

Ginger: Reduces cold symptoms, reduces inflammation, nausea, and symptoms in respiratory illnesses. Also used along with other medications to help with diseases like cancer.

Lemongrass: Has anti-fungal, anti-bacterial, and anti-microbial properties

Hibiscus: Reduces cold symptoms, and even shown to have anti-cancer properties.

Licorice Root: Soothes gastrointestinal problems, reduces cold sumptoms, and ha antiviral and anti-microbial properties to prevent illness.

Blueberry: Reduces body inflammation, and overall inflammation.

Green: Prevents disease and has antiinflammatory, antiviral, and antibacterial properties

Marshmallow Root: Relieves pain, and inflammation, especially in the throat area.

Clove: Reduce headache pains, and have antimicrobial and antiviral properties.

Tumeric: Relieves migraines and headaches.

Lavender: Reduces migrains and headaches, while relieving anxiety and depression.

Important Notes!

  • Stay hydrated! Drink water multiple times throughout the day to help with your illness.
  • You can mix in honey with any of the teas, or with water to help soothe a sore throat. HOWEVER, this should only be done with teenagers, and adults.
  • Chicken broths, fruits high in vitamin c, and zinc are helpful towards illness.
  • Keep the air purified, and clean.
  • Humidity, steam, and hot showers help with mucus.
