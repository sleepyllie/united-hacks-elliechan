About this Page

  • This site is designed for helping individuals with finding the right over-the-counter medications for them, to reduce uncomfortableness when ill.

  • Of course, this does not mean you should not consult a doctor. If possible, please do.

  • In fact, this site was designed in the case that a doctor's visit may be too expensive, or if the illness is immediately treatable!

  • It is especially important to keep in mind that bacterial infections that are not treated can damage an individual's health further, and cannot be treated by any over the counter means.

  • Please also remember to keep in mind that if you are noticably sick, distance from others is a good idea until no longer contagious (usually until fever breaks, or 24hours is over).
